What Happens on a Service Call?

Get a service call which includes travel to your home and the diagnosis when the repair is performed. We charge by the job, not by the hour, TO SAVE YOU MONEY!!

1 You call or fill out a form to schedule an appointment.

2 Our HVAC technician will call you 30 minutes prior to arriving.

3 Our technician arrives, tests the system and fully diagnoses the problem all within the first half hour.

4 We review all repair options with you and answer any questions you may have.

5 We give you a quote based on the job, not by the hour to save you money!

6 Once approved, we get to work on the repair and test it thoroughly. 

7 We won't expect the balance due until the inspector has passed the installation.

8 You're good to go. Enjoy the comfort of your own home!
